01 Strategy and Policy

We facilitate government ministries and agencies to develop
policies and strategies. We are experts in training and facilitation of

  • Corporate strategic planning
  • ICT strategy & policy formulation
  • Knowledge Management strategy & policy formulation
  • Change management strategy & policy
  • Data privacy strategy & policy
  • Corporate Governance
  • Scenario planning

02 Project Management and Evaluation

We are experts in project assessments and training of project implementers.

Project Evaluation
  • Project baseline surveys
  • Mid-term evaluation
  • End-term evaluation
  • Impact evaluation

Training in Project management and evaluation
  • Project management training using MS or Open office
  • Training of trainers in community engagement strategy
  • Training of trainers in project monitoring and evaluation
  • Training of trainers in grant proposal writing

03 ICT

We facilitate ICT and knowledge workers to keep abreast with emerging technologies. Informed workers are able to review their ICT frameworks for better organizational performance.

  • Training in Data Science application in policy and strategy
  • Training in Devops
  • Training in Cascading the ICT strategy from the Corporate Strategic Plan

04 ISO management systems

We train implementers and auditors of ISO Management Systems and connect them with industry for practice and mentorship.

Project Evaluation
  • ISO 9001 Quality Management System
  • ISO 22001 Food Safety Management System
  • Environmental Management System
  • ISO 27001 Information Security Management System
  • ISO/IEC 17025 Lab Management System

About Us

We are a leading consulting firm specializing in utilizing innovative and emerging technologies to provide informed policy, strategic, and project management solutions. Our expertise lies in applying well-established consulting tools, frameworks, and best practices to assist clients in navigating intricate strategic and operational challenges. With a focus on practicality and competency-based training, our programs offer hands-on learning experiences. Furthermore, our global network allows us to deliver context-specific trainings tailored to the unique needs of our clients.
